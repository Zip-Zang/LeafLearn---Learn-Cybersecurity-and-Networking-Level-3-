📁 Employer Set Project

Spring Leaf Digital Infrastructure — your graded ESP assessment. Sat under supervised conditions, no internet, no AI. Use this section to understand every task, walk through the steps, and feel confident going in.

⏱ Total: ~14.5 hours across 5 tasks 🎯 Total: 103 marks
🏢
⚠️ Important: In the actual assessment you are NOT permitted internet access or AI tools. Use this website to prepare and understand the tasks — not during the exam itself.
🗺️ The Scenario

Spring Leaf is a UK sales & marketing support company expanding its operations. They have existing offices in London and Leeds, and are opening a new Edinburgh office. Across all 5 tasks you play the role of a digital support technician managing this infrastructure upgrade end-to-end.

  • Task 1 — Plan the project (Gantt chart, costs, rationale)
  • Task 2 — Diagnose and fix a broken network in Packet Tracer
  • Task 3 — Produce a needs analysis for the Leeds office
  • Task 4a — Build and extend the Leeds network in Packet Tracer
  • Task 4b — Write a reflective evaluation of your Task 4a solution
